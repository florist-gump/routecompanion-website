1. Introduction

Route Companion ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

Route Companion is designed to be privacy-first. We do not operate backend servers and do not store your personal data remotely.

  • Route Data: Routes you import (GPX files, Strava, Komoot, Ride with GPS) are processed and stored locally on your device only.
  • POI Data: Points of interest are fetched from OpenStreetMap (via the Overpass API) and cached locally on your device.
  • Authentication Tokens: If you connect third-party services (Strava, Komoot, Ride with GPS), OAuth access tokens are stored securely on your device using the OS-provided secure storage (Keychain on iOS).
  • Location Data: Your location is used for navigation, proximity alerts, and Live Activity display. Location data is processed on-device and never sent to our servers.
  • Preferences: App settings such as unit system, map type, POI filters, and route appearance are stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Data

All data processing happens on your device. We use data solely to:

  • Display your routes and points of interest on the map.
  • Provide turn-by-turn navigation and proximity alerts.
  • Display Live Activity information on your iOS Lock Screen.
  • Export routes with POIs to FIT, TCX, and GPX formats for use with GPS devices.
  • Import routes from connected third-party services.

4. Third-Party Services

Route Companion integrates with the following services. When you use these integrations, your data is also subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API: For fetching POI data along your routes. We send geographic queries (coordinates and search radius) to retrieve nearby points of interest.
  • Strava API: For importing your saved routes (optional). We request read-only access to your routes. Subject to Strava's Privacy Policy.
  • Komoot API: For importing your planned tours (optional). Subject to Komoot's Privacy Policy.
  • Ride with GPS API: For importing your routes (optional). Subject to RWGPS Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Maps / Google Maps: For map tiles and base map display. Subject to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

5. Data Storage & Security

All route data, POIs, preferences, and history are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage where available. We do not have access to any of your data. No data is transmitted to servers operated by us.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. The only network requests made by the app are to the third-party services listed above, and only when you explicitly initiate an action (importing a route, searching for POIs).

7. Analytics & Tracking

We do not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, or any form of telemetry. We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking pixels.

8. Children's Privacy

Route Companion does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information to function.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@routecompanion.app.
