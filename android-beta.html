Route Companion

Android Beta

Join the Android test program

Help test Route Companion on Android before the public Play Store launch. The beta uses Google Groups for tester access, then Google Play for installation and updates.

1

Join the Google Group

Use the same Google account that you use on your Android device. Google Play uses this group membership to recognize you as a tester.

group_add Join Google Group
2

Join the Play test

After joining the group, open the Play testing page and opt in with the same Google account.

fact_check Join Play Test
3

Install from Google Play

Once opted in, open the Google Play listing on Android and install the latest test build.

android Open Google Play

If Google Play says the app is unavailable, make sure you joined the group with the same account used on your Android device. For help, email support@routecompanion.app.
